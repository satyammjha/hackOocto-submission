    ABOUT US!

    A uniquely designed "CleanItUp" is a cutting-edge web application dedicated to revolutionizing waste management through a combination of technology and community engagement. Our mission is to foster responsible waste disposal practices, enhance civic participation, and create a cleaner, more sustainable environment for all.
